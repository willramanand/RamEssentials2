# RamEssentials Permissions

Administrative:

- `ramessentials.admin` - `/ramessentials`, `/re`
- `ramessentials.economy.admin` - `/economy`, `/eco`
- `ramessentials.warp.admin` - `/warp setpermission`, `/warp category`, `/warp icon`

Teleport and movement:

- `ramessentials.back` - `/back`
- `ramessentials.spawn` - `/spawn`
- `ramessentials.world` - `/world`
- `ramessentials.home` - `/home`
- `ramessentials.home.rename` - `/home rename`
- `ramessentials.sethome` - `/sethome`
- `ramessentials.delhome` - `/delhome`
- `ramessentials.warp` - `/warp`
- `ramessentials.warps` - `/warps`
- `ramessentials.warp.info` - `/warp info`
- `ramessentials.setwarp` - `/setwarp`
- `ramessentials.delwarp` - `/delwarp`
- `ramessentials.tpa` - `/tpa`
- `ramessentials.tpahere` - `/tpahere`
- `ramessentials.tpaccept` - `/tpaccept`
- `ramessentials.tpdeny` - `/tpdeny`
- `ramessentials.teleport.bypass-warmup` - bypass teleport warmups
- `ramessentials.cooldown.bypass` - bypass all configured cooldowns
- `ramessentials.<command>.bypass-cooldown` - bypass one cooldown, such as `ramessentials.home.bypass-cooldown`

Homes:

- `ramessentials.homes` - `/homes`
- `ramessentials.homes.unlimited` - unlimited homes
- `ramessentials.homes.limit.<key>` - use configured home limit from `homes.permission-limits.<key>`

Player utilities:

- `ramessentials.balance` - `/balance`
- `ramessentials.balancetop` - `/balancetop`
- `ramessentials.broadcast` - `/broadcast`
- `ramessentials.god` - `/god`
- `ramessentials.donotdisturb` - `/donotdisturb`, `/dnd`
- `ramessentials.gamemode` - `/gamemode`
- `ramessentials.gamemode.others` - change another player's gamemode
- `ramessentials.nickname` - `/nickname`, `/nick`
- `ramessentials.message` - `/message`, `/msg`
- `ramessentials.reply` - `/reply`, `/r`
- `ramessentials.ignore` - `/ignore`
- `ramessentials.fly` - `/fly`
- `ramessentials.fly.others` - toggle another player's flight
- `ramessentials.flyspeed` - `/flyspeed`
- `ramessentials.heal` - `/heal`
- `ramessentials.heal.others` - heal another player
- `ramessentials.pay` - `/pay`
